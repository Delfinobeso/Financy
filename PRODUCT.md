# Financy — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Personal monthly budget tracker for Italian freelancers and workers. Helps users allocate income across categories, track daily spending, close months with savings rollover, and build a piggy bank over time. PWA, mobile-first, works offline.

## Register
product

## Users
Primary: Italian freelancer or worker aged 25-40, managing variable income. Uses the app on iPhone in the evening to check if they can afford something. Cares about accuracy over aesthetics, but a polished interface builds trust. Secondary: anyone in Italy managing a personal budget.

## Scene
A Milan-based designer checks expenses at 11pm on the couch, phone at half brightness. They want to know in 3 seconds if they're within budget. No friction, no learning curve, no fluff.

## Brand
Financy. Calm, precise, trustworthy. Not flashy finance-bro. Not sterile corporate. Somewhere between Notion and Monzo: considered typography, restrained color, moments of warmth. Italian language throughout.

## Anti-references
- Crypto apps (neon on black, aggressive gradients)
- Traditional banking apps (navy + gold, stuffy)
- Generic "budgeting app" with pie charts and confetti
- Material Design 3 with colorful surfaces everywhere

## Strategic principles
1. Speed over decoration — every interaction should resolve in under 2 taps
2. Numbers first — financial data is the content; UI is the container
3. Month as the atomic unit — all flows orient around the monthly cycle
4. Trust through consistency — same vocabulary everywhere
